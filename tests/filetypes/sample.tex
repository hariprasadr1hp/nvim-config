\section{Mathematics}
Inline math can be written as $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$, or for larger equations:
\begin{equation}
    E = mc^2
\end{equation}

You can also create aligned equations:
\begin{align}
    x &= y + z \\
    a &= b + c
\end{align}
